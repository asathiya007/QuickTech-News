About Us

QuickTech Blogs was started by Akshay Sathiya in February 2019. This project was inspired by Sathiya's noticing of himself and many others' struggles to stay updated and in the loop with the ever so quickly developing and growing tech industry.

Realizing this, Sathiya decided to build this website, which would use his unique "three sets of three" approach on important, current events in the tech industry. In three bullet points, the event will be summarized. In three more bullet points, the implications of the event will be stated. The last three bullet points will point the reader to other sites/resources for them to read more, if they wish.

This approach will enable many more people to keep up with and understand the tech industry to a much greater degree.
